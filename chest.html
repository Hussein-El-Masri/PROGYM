CHEST EXERCISES

Exercise 1: Bench Press

  • | The bench press, or chest press, is a weight training exercise where a person presses a weight upwards while lying horizontally on a weight training bench.

Exercise 2: Dumbbell Bench Press

  • | Dumbbell Bench Press. Extremely similar to the first chest exercise, the dumbbell bench press more accurately follows the intended function of the pectoral.

Exercise 3: Incline Bench Press

  • | The incline bench press is a variation of the bench press and an exercise used to build the muscles of the chest. The shoulders and triceps will be indirectly involved as well. Utilizing an incline will allow you to better target the upper portion of the chest, a lagging part for a lot of lifters.
